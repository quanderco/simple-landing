What we're building

The infrastructure of a company factory.

Quander is an applied AI product lab. We are building agents for validating, building, and scaling businesses - making it easy for anyone to create and run a company. Not thousands of companies for a handful of people. Millions of companies, for millions of people, worldwide.

We want more people to be more free, more intelligent, more empowered, and happier. That is the real measure of what we're building.

How it works

The Quander system.

Our system is a set of AI agents - value-creation nodes - each of which automates a critical function in the business lifecycle. Together they form a closed loop: discover, build, test, and scale.

We're starting with distribution, because without customers, nothing else matters.

Node 01 - live

Hypesonic: AI distribution agent

Hypesonic runs marketing and distribution continuously - testing creative, targeting audiences, and optimizing spend without human bottlenecks. It doesn't sleep, doesn't guess, and gets better over time.

Customers include Kumon, Cloaked, and Mito Health, with direct increases in ROAS of 50% to 500%.

Node 02 - in development

Market discovery agent

Analyzes online forums and discussions to surface real pain points, quantify unmet demand, and identify business ideas grounded in actual market signals - not assumptions.

Node 03 - in development

Technology PM agent

Sources and communicates with engineers and designers to ship products end-to-end, looping in a human at the right moments in the product lifecycle.

As new opportunities appear across the business lifecycle, we will build additional nodes. The goal is a system that can discover, build, and scale companies repeatedly - and autonomously.

The bigger picture

A new model of economic expansion.

In the short term, we support other companies with our products to generate revenue and fuel our growth. In the long term, we build our own portfolio - an ever-growing set of companies, each addressing unmet demand with shared intelligence.

But the real goal isn't the portfolio. It's what the portfolio enables: more people running their own businesses, building what they love, living on their own terms. Automation of the mundane creates room for what actually matters - leisure, family, creativity, exploration.
